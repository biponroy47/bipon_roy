Bipon Roy is an 18 year old Canadian photographer and aspiring filmmaker based in Toronto, Ontario. He currently attends the University of Western Ontario majoring and specializing in Computer Science.

Bipon began shooting at the age of 14 where he then explored the streets of Toronto in search of different perspectives of the city. He primarily shoots landscapes of the city skyline but also enjoys shooting portraits when he is not climbing rooftops.
